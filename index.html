PUBLICATIONS


  • Text2Avatar: Text to 3D Human Avatar Generation with Codebook-Driven Body Controllable Attribute, ICASSP2024 (IF 12.04)

    Text2Avatar is the first model that can generate realistic-style 3D avatars based on complex and coupled input text prompts, achieving multiattribute controllable and realistic 3D human avatar generation.

    • Related area: Cross-modal Generation, Decoupling Control, 3D Avatar Reconstruction, NeRF Generation.

  • EXPLORING MULTI-MODAL CONTROL IN MUSIC-DRIVEN DANCE GENERATION, ICASSP2024 (IF 12.04)

    We propose a unified framework capable of generating high-quality dance movements and supporting multi-modal control, including genre control, semantic control, and spatial control simultaneously.

    • Related area: Human Motion Controllability, Human Motion Generation, SMPL, Cross-Modal Generation, Multi-media.

  • 基于Encoder-Decoder注意力网络的异常驾驶行为在线识别方法，兵器工程学报2023（IF 0.87）

    We proposed a framework for real-time monitoring of drivers’ abnormal driving behavior. Unlike existing methods that rely on a large number of expensive and high-precision sensors, we only use mobile phone data for abnormal driving behavior detection, making our method more practical.

    • Related area: Abnormal driving, Deep Learning, LSTM, Attention Mechanism.

  • 转动惯量平行轴定理验证实验的改进方案，大学物理2020（IF 0.33）

    We have found a method that is more accurate compared to the current teaching method, which can significantly reduce the amount of error introduced during the measurement of the moment of inertia of rigid bodies in experiments. This will help college students to conduct experiments more effectively and gain a better understanding of the experimental principles.

    • Related area: Moment of inertia of rigid bodies, experimental physics.
